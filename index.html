Classic Vanilla Cake

Prep: 20m • Bake: 30m
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, stir until smooth.
  3. Bake 28–32 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep: 15m • Bake: 10m
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add flour, fold in chips.
  3. Scoop onto tray and bake 9–12 minutes.
🧁

Fun Frosted Cupcakes

Prep: 15m • Bake: 18m
Ingredients:
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Frosting of choice
Instructions:
  1. Mix batter, fill cupcake liners 2/3 full.
  2. Bake 16–20 minutes at 350°F (175°C).
  3. Cool and decorate with frosting and sprinkles.
🍞

Simple Homemade Bread

Prep: 15m • Rise: 1h • Bake: 30m
Ingredients:
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
Instructions:
  1. Mix yeast with warm water, add flour and salt, knead into dough.
  2. Let rise 45–60 minutes, shape, then bake 30–35 minutes at 375°F (190°C).

Fudgy Brownies

Prep: 10m • Bake: 25m
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 eggs
Instructions:
  1. Mix wet ingredients, stir in dry ingredients until combined.
  2. Pour into pan and bake 22–28 minutes at 350°F (175°C).

Baked Donuts with Glaze

Prep: 20m • Bake: 10m
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Glaze (powdered sugar + milk)
Instructions:
  1. Mix batter, pipe into donut pan, bake 8–10 minutes at 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cool and dip into glaze.
